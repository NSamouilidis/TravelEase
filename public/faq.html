Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TravelEase services

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. Fill in your username, email address, and create a password. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, then click "Create Account". You'll be ready to explore and book trips in just a few seconds.

How do I book a trip?

To book a trip, first browse our destinations or itineraries and select one that interests you. On the destination detail page, you'll find a booking form where you can select your preferred transportation, travel dates, and number of travelers. Fill out the form, add any special requests, and click "Book Now". You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details of your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. We also support PayPal for secure online payments. All payments are processed through a secure payment gateway to ensure your financial information remains safe.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking by going to your profile page and selecting "My Bookings". Find the booking you wish to change and use the "Cancel" or "Modify" options. Please note that cancellation policies vary by destination, and some bookings may incur fees if changed close to the travel date. Full details are provided at the time of booking.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we pride ourselves on transparency. The price you see is the final price you'll pay, with all taxes and fees included. We clearly display the breakdown of costs before you confirm your booking so you know exactly what you're paying for. There are no surprise charges or hidden fees.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer optional travel insurance for all bookings. During the checkout process, you'll have the option to add comprehensive travel insurance that covers trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. We recommend adding insurance to protect your travel investment, especially for international trips.

What's included in the itinerary packages?

Our itinerary packages typically include accommodation, transportation between destinations, guided tours at key attractions, and some meals as specified in the itinerary description. Each package is different, so please check the detailed inclusions listed on the itinerary page. Additional services like airport transfers or optional activities may be available at an extra cost.

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7. You can reach us by email at support@travelease.com, by phone at +44 7483 192547, or through the Contact form on our website. For urgent matters while traveling, we provide an emergency contact number in your booking confirmation.

Didn't find your answer?

Our customer service team is ready to help you with any questions you may have.
